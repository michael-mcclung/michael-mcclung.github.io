Plant Placement Analyzer (PPA)

Will tell user if a certain plant will survive in the area by measuring different readings given by four sensors. The sensors are luminosity to measure the light given by the sun or any other light sources, humidity to measure the concentration of water wapour present in the air, soil moisture to measure the water stored in the soil and how it is affected by precipitation and some other characteristics, and finally temperature to measure how hot or cold it is. All was programmed in the language of C++.
